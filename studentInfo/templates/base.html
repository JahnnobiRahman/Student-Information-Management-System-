Academics History

Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology, commonly known as RUET (রুয়েট), is one of the leading PhD granting public research universities in Bangladesh. It is one of the most prestigious public universities in the country that specialises in the field of engineering. RUET was founded in 1964 as Rajshahi Engineering College with a limited number of students. It was then changed to Bangladesh Institute of Technology (BIT) in 1986 and was finally renamed Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET) in 2003 when it gained a university status.

At present, more than 3500 students are pursuing their higher study in this green campus including under-graduate and post-graduate with over 250 prominent faculty members in diverse field of expertise. The medium of instruction and necessary assessment of this university is English.
